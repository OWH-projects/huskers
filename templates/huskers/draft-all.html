Huskers in the NFL Draft

Nebraska has an NFL draft history that dates back to the beginning: 1936, when Bernie Scherer was selected 13th overall by the Green Bay Packers. Two Huskers have been selected with the No. 1 pick — Sam Francis in 1937 and Irving Fryar in 1984. The full list features some statistical oddities, too: The 1984 draft includes three Huskers who were picked during a special supplemental draft, while two Huskers — Tom Novak and Charley Toogood — were selected in two different drafts.
